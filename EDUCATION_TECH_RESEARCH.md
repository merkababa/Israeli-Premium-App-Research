## IDEA 1: Bagrut + Psychometric AI-Powered Test Prep Platform

### 1. What It Is and How It Works

A Hebrew-language, mobile-first platform offering AI-adaptive test preparation for Israel's two highest-stakes exams: the **Bagrut (matriculation exams)** and the **Psychometric Entrance Test (PET)**. The platform combines:

- **AI-adaptive practice engine**: Students answer questions in Hebrew, and the system identifies weak areas and adjusts difficulty in real time, using spaced repetition algorithms
- **Video micro-lessons** (5-10 minutes each): Covering every Bagrut subject (Math 3-5 units, English, Bible, History, Civics) and all three PET domains (Verbal, Quantitative, English)
- **Full-length timed simulations**: Authentic exam simulations with score prediction and detailed analytics
- **AI tutor chatbot**: Hebrew-language AI assistant that explains solutions step-by-step, powered by LLM with custom fine-tuning on Israeli curriculum content
- **Progress dashboard for parents**: Real-time visibility into their child's preparation progress
- **Weekly study plans**: Auto-generated based on exam date and current proficiency level

The platform differentiates from existing players (Kidum, Yoel Geva) by being **fully digital, AI-adaptive, and significantly cheaper** than traditional classroom courses.

### 7. Sustainability Probability: 72%

**Rationale**: The Israeli test prep market is a proven NIS 1.3B industry with predictable annual demand cycles. Digital disruption is inevitable. The AI-adaptive approach creates genuine learning value that builds word-of-mouth. The key risk is content quality requiring ongoing investment, but once the question bank reaches critical mass (2,000+ per subject), the marginal cost of serving additional students approaches zero. Recurring exam cycles (Bagrut annually, PET quarterly) ensure repeat cohorts.

### 8. Failure Probability: 28%

**Key Risks**:
- **Content quality barrier**: Hebrew-language, curriculum-aligned content requires expert educators; poor quality = no retention
- **Government competition**: The free Campus.gov.il psychometric course undermines pricing power for PET prep
- **eSelf AI tutor pilot**: [Israel's nationwide AI tutoring rollout with eSelf](https://www.calcalistech.com/ctechnews/article/r1tkoce1gg) could commoditize AI tutoring
- **Cultural resistance**: Israeli parents may still prefer in-person tutors for high-stakes exams
- **Customer acquisition cost**: Marketing to Hebrew-speaking teens requires expensive social media spend (Instagram, TikTok)

### 9. Why Israel Specifically Needs This

- **80% of school principals report teacher shortages**, especially in math and English
- **Only ~50% of students pass the Bagrut** -- massive demand for affordable, accessible preparation
- **Current prep costs NIS 2,000-6,000/month** -- economically excludes lower-income families
- **No existing Hebrew-language AI adaptive platform** -- a clear technology gap
- **Israel has the highest youth population ratio in OECD** (34.3% of population are students)
- **Rising cost of living crisis** makes cheaper digital alternatives increasingly attractive

## IDEA 2: Hebrew-Language Tutoring Marketplace (Bagrut + Psychometric Focus)

### 1. What It Is and How It Works

A **two-sided marketplace** connecting Israeli students with verified tutors for **1-on-1 and small-group online sessions**, focused exclusively on the Israeli education system. Think "Preply/Superprof for Israel":

- **Tutor profiles** with verified credentials, Bagrut/PET expertise, student ratings, and video introductions
- **Smart matching algorithm**: Matches students with tutors based on subject, difficulty level (3/4/5 units), budget, schedule, and learning style
- **Integrated video classroom**: Built-in whiteboard, screen sharing, and session recording
- **Automated scheduling and payments**: Calendar sync, NIS payment processing, automatic invoicing
- **Parent oversight panel**: Parents book, pay, and monitor session quality and student progress
- **Subject focus**: All Bagrut subjects (Math, English, Hebrew, Bible, History, Sciences) + Psychometric prep
