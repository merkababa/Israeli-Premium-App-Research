# Business Plan & Strategy

## Core Value Proposition (CVP)
**"Time is your most valuable asset. We curate and deliver vetted premium services to your door with zero friction."**
- **For Users:** No more scrolling through Facebook groups or calling providers. One tap to book a trusted, high-quality service provider.
- **For Providers:** Access to a high-paying clientele without spending money on marketing or dealing with scheduling headaches.

## Outline MVP Features
Focus on doing one vertical perfectly (e.g., Premium Home Services) before expanding.
1. **User Authentication:** Phone number login via SMS (standard in Israel) or Apple/Google Sign-In.
2. **AI Search & Discovery:** A clean interface to browse 3–4 highly vetted service categories, supplemented by an AI chat search (e.g., "I need a deep clean for a 4-room apartment in Herzliya tomorrow morning").
3. **Booking Engine:** Calendar integration to select available timeslots for providers.
4. **Frictionless Checkout:** Apple Pay and Google Pay integration (Israeli high-income earners use digital wallets heavily). Credit card support via Stripe.
5. **Provider Dashboard:** A simple web view or app section for service providers to accept jobs, manage schedules, and see their earnings.

## Monetization Strategy
- **Primary:** **Take Rate (Commission).** Charge a 10–15% commission on the provider side for every successful booking. The user sees a flat, transparent price.
- **Secondary (Post-MVP):** **Premium Subscription.** Introduce a "Pro" tier (e.g., 39 NIS/month) for users that offers waived service fees, priority booking, and exclusive curated experiences.

## User Acquisition Channels
- **B2B2C Partnerships (High ROI):** Partner with HR departments of Israeli tech companies. Offer their employees a "perk" (e.g., 100 NIS off their first premium booking). Acquires high-income users in bulk efficiently.
- **Influencer Marketing:** Partner with Israeli micro-influencers on Instagram and TikTok focusing on lifestyle, home decor, and tech.
- **Hyper-Local Social Ads:** Geo-targeted Meta (Facebook/Instagram) ads focusing strictly on wealthy neighborhoods in Tel Aviv, Herzliya, and Savyon.
- **SEO & Content:** Create localized content in Hebrew targeting specific long-tail keywords (e.g., "vetted private chef Tel Aviv").

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):** Cost to acquire a new booking user (needs to be kept low via B2B2C partnerships).
- **Lifetime Value (LTV):** Total revenue expected from a user; repeat usage is key.
- **Repeat Booking Rate:** Percentage of users who book a second service within 30 days (crucial for product-market fit).
- **Gross Merchandise Value (GMV):** Total value of services booked through the platform.
- **Take Rate Margin:** Actual revenue after paying the service providers.
